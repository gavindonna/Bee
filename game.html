Bee Game

To play the game, you have to guess what the word is. You guess by picking letters and seeing if they are in the word. However, if you pick a letter that is not in the word a man is slowly drawn. With each wrong letter guess the man is drawn more and more. When the man is finished, he is "hung" and the game is lost. All words are bee-related
